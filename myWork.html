DE AFDELING

Outside my study I ran a student bar, from january 2024 till january 2026, together with a team of driven students from all different disciplines. During this time my task was to change and refresh Café de Afdeling’s visual identity and setup a ‘design system’ for future students so they a standard to work with.

As Promotions, I introduced and shaped the role yourself, giving the bar a unified and evolving visual identity. You manage both digital (social media, fast responses) and physical (posters, flyers, designs) promotions, ensuring the bar remains professional, relevant, and vibrant.

I emphasized inclusivity in all communications—making the bar’s identity open, welcoming, and accessible, while also protecting it as a neutral space free of divisive or alienating messages.

  1. Organized & Reliable → keeping communication structured and efficient.
  2. Creative and Cohesive → crafting and evolving a cohesive brand identity.
  3. Initiative-Taker → creating the Promotions role and shaping its importance.
  4. Community-Minded → seeing the bar as a living, breathing hub that adapts with its people.
  5. Inclusive & Responsible → ensuring designs and messages are welcoming to all.
  6. Professional & Consistent → balancing creativity with clarity and reliability.
