MY WORKS:

DE AFDELING

Outside my study I ran a student bar, from january 2024 till january 2026, together with a team of driven students from all different disciplines. During this time my task was to change and refresh Café de Afdeling’s visual identity and setup a ‘design system’ for future students so they have a standard to work with.

NOTICING WATER

During your Minor in Creative Research, I explored a wide range of research methods, both established and self-developed, applying them to different topics.

REFRESH MEDIA

For about 3–4 months, I worked at Refresh Media as an intern, a small but long-established company, where I contributed to designing UX websites, web applications, UIs, and visuals.

WHOPPAH

In my studies of Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD), the focus lies in creating digital interfaces while keeping the user experience central in every outcome.

GITANOS BLYNAI

Gitanos Blynai is a beloved family-owned crêperie in Lithuania, recognized locally and awarded as one of the best family-run businesses in their town.

ESI TANALA

As part of my Immersive Environments minor, I created an individual project for the exhibition titled “Esi Tanala” (They Who Gaze). In this project I researched the topic of Intergenerational trauma of Moluccan people in the Netherlands through the eyes of my family.

Front-End Development

During my studies I took part in the course Front-End Development, which focussed on HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The project allowed me to dive deeper in to Accessibility, Responsive Design and overall improving my skills and comfort in FED.
