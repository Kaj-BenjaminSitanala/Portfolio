ABOUT ME

I am a visual, UX/UI designer and researcher, studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (HvA).
With an empathetic approach and a research-driven mindset, I focus on shaping impactful brands and intuitive digital experiences. My aim is to bridge creativity and functionality, turning ideas into designs that connect with you.
